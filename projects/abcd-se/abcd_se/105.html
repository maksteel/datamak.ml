Component 2 : A very approximately periodic function with a period of 1.0 years
This component is assumed to continue to be approximately periodic. The shape of the function is
assumed to vary smoothly between periods but will quickly return to the prior. The prior is entirely
uncertain about the phase of the periodic function. Consequently the pointwise posterior will appear
to lose its periodicity, but this merely reflects the uncertainty in the shape and phase of the function.

4
Model checking
Several posterior predictive checks have been performed to assess how well the model describes
the observed data.
These tests take the form of comparing statistics evaluated on samples from
the prior and posterior distributions for each additive component. The statistics are derived from
autocorrelation function (ACF) estimates, periodograms and quantile-quantile (qq) plots.
