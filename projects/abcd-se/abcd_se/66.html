Experiments with CV SoD method
We performed these experiments to evaluate, the amount of variation in the prediction
accuracy, when model is discovered using, a novel CV SoD model evaluation method
as described in Section
4.2.2
. We used “balanced design” experimentation approach i.e.
combinations of model search strategy (greedy and top-k) and model scoring criterion
(BIC and AIC), resulted in four set of experiments (see Table
4.1
).
We collectively analyse the results of CV SoD method (this section) and Hybrid SoD
method (Section
5.2
) experiments in Section
5.4
.
