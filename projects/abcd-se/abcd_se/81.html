Conclusion
In this section, we relate our results back to the contributions that were stated in the
beginning of this dissertation. We examine the evidence obtained from the results of our
experiments to show how each of our contributions are supported. We also draw out
attention to any of our aims that were not supported by our experiments and limitations
within our techniques. We discuss future work required to further validate the claims about
providing stable extrapolation with high interpretability, made by CV SoD evaluation
method.
Finally, we reflect on the work we have done, considering the context of its subject area.
We explore why and how our work is an important contribution to a rapidly developing,
important and incredibly useful field.
