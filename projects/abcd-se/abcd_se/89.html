Component 2 : An approximately periodic function with a period of 1.0 years
This component is approximately periodic with a period of 1.0 years. Across periods the shape
of this function varies very smoothly. The shape of this function within each period has a typical
lengthscale of 2.2 months.
This component explains 94.8% of the residual variance; this increases the total variance explained
from 85.3% to 99.2%. The addition of this component reduces the cross validated MAE by 67.98%
from 30.67 to 9.82.
